‚≠ê Phase 3 Priority: Purchasing Kits

This is the feature that makes realtors choose LO Buddy over competitors.

What is a Purchasing Kit?

A beautiful, branded PDF or shareable link that realtors give to listing agents when submitting an offer. Think of it as a "professional buyer presentation package."

üéØ The Value Proposition
  • For Realtors: Makes their offers look stronger and more professional
  • For Listing Agents: Easy-to-digest summary that proves the buyer is qualified
  • For LOs: Marketing material with their branding on every offer

What's Included in a Purchasing Kit:

  • ‚úÖ Borrower names (personalized)
  • ‚úÖ Pre-approval range ($450K - $525K, for example)
  • ‚úÖ Loan type (Conventional, FHA, VA, etc.)
  • ‚úÖ Down payment amount/percentage
  • ‚úÖ Estimated closing timeline
  • ‚úÖ LO contact info + branding
  • ‚úÖ Professional design with charts/visuals
  • ‚ùå NO real credit scores
  • ‚ùå NO DTI ratios
  • ‚ùå NO actual financial documents

Key insight: It looks impressive but contains only what you'd share in an offer letter anyway. The magic is in the presentation ‚Äî making the buyer look serious and backed by a professional team.

Technical Implementation:

  • New API endpoint: /api/realtor/purchasing-kits
  • PDF generation with Next.js + Puppeteer or React-PDF
  • Shareable public link (no auth required): /purchasing-kit/[shareId]
  • Template selection (modern, classic, minimal styles)
  • Auto-populate from opportunity data
  • Download as PDF or share link via text/email

‚≠ê Merge Refi Machine Logic

The Refi Machine (Martin Weinstein's creation) has sophisticated refinance analysis. We're bringing that power into LO Buddy's scenario engine.

Refi Machine Features to Integrate:

  • First + Second Lien Comparison: Show how a second mortgage compares to refinancing
  • Credit Card Debt Consolidation: Roll high-interest debt into mortgage, show monthly savings
  • Cash-Out Refi Analysis: Pull equity for home improvements, show payback period
  • Rate & Term Refi: Lower rate, shorter term, both ‚Äî show all options
  • Break-Even Calculator: "How long until closing costs are recovered?"
  • Payment vs. Equity Chart: Visualize how payment changes affect equity buildup

Example Scenario: Client has $30K in credit card debt at 22% APR. Refi Machine shows:

  • Current monthly payment: $900 (cards) + $2,200 (mortgage) = $3,100
  • After cash-out refi: $2,650 (new mortgage) = $450/month savings
  • Break-even: 14 months
  • Total interest saved over 5 years: $87,000

‚≠ê AI Conversational Scenario Builder

The AI agent can build scenarios from natural language. No more clicking through forms ‚Äî just talk.

Example Interactions:

User: "Run a scenario for John at 6.5% with 10% down on a $500K purchase"

Agent: "Got it. I'm building a purchase scenario for John with these parameters:

  • Purchase price: $500,000
  • Down payment: 10% ($50,000)
  • Interest rate: 6.5%
  • Loan term: 30 years (assumed)
Estimated monthly payment: $2,845 (PITI). Should I save this?"

User: "Compare that to 15% down"

Agent: "Created comparison scenario:

  • 10% down: $2,845/mo | $50K upfront | PMI: $187/mo
  • 15% down: $2,723/mo | $75K upfront | PMI: $94/mo
Savings: $122/month, but needs $25K more upfront. Break-even: 204 months. Which should we go with?"

Technical Approach:

  • Agent function: create_scenario(params)
  • Parse natural language ‚Üí structured params
  • Auto-fill sensible defaults (30-year term, etc.)
  • Confirm with user before saving
  • Store in database with "created_by_agent" flag
