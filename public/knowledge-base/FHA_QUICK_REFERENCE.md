# FHA Quick Reference Guide (HUD 4000.1)

## Income Documentation (II.A.4)

### Employment Income (II.A.4.c.ii)
- Most recent 30-day paystub + most recent 2 years W-2s
- VOE acceptable in lieu of paystub
- **Gaps in employment:** Must be documented; gaps > 6 months in past 2 years require LOE

### Self-Employment (II.A.4.c.ii)
- **2 years tax returns required** (no exceptions — stricter than Fannie)
- Must own ≥ 25% of business
- 2-year average is mandatory
- If business < 2 years: ineligible for SE income
- IRS 4506-C (tax transcript) required

### Variable Income (II.A.4.c.iii)
- Overtime/Bonus: 2-year history, likely to continue
- Commission: 2-year history required
- Declining trend: must use lower amount or document sustainability

### Rental Income (II.A.4.c.xii)
- **75% of gross rent** minus PITIA
- Net positive = added to income
- Net negative = added to liabilities
- Self-sufficiency test for 3-4 units: 75% of gross rent must ≥ PITIA

### Other Income
| Type | Reference | Key Rule |
|---|---|---|
| Alimony/Child Support | II.A.4.c.viii | Must continue ≥ 3 years; 12-month receipt history |
| Social Security | II.A.4.c.xi | Award letter; gross up non-taxable by 25% (if tax-exempt docs provided) |
| Disability | II.A.4.c.xi | Must be ≥ 3 years continuance likely |
| Part-time | II.A.4.c.iv | 2-year history, uninterrupted |
| Seasonal | II.A.4.c.iv | 2-year history, must show pattern |
| Non-occupant co-borrower | II.A.4.d.vi | Allowed for family members; their income used for qualifying |

## DTI Requirements (II.A.4.a)

| Scenario | Max DTI |
|---|---|
| TOTAL Scorecard Accept | Per AUS (typically up to 56.99%) |
| Manual Underwrite | See matrix below |

### Manual Underwrite DTI Matrix
| Credit Score | Max DTI (no compensating factors) | Max DTI (with compensating factors) |
|---|---|---|
| ≥ 680 | 31/43 | 37/47 |
| 620-679 | 31/43 | 37/47 |
| 580-619 | 31/43 | Not eligible for stretch |
| < 580 | Not eligible | Not eligible |

### Compensating Factors (Manual)
- Verified reserves (≥ 3 months)
- Minimal housing payment increase
- Residual income per VA tables
- No discretionary debt
- Conservative use of credit
- Significant additional income not used for qualifying

## LTV/CLTV Limits (II.A.2)

### Purchase
| Property | LTV |
|---|---|
| 1-4 unit Primary (FICO ≥ 580) | 96.5% |
| 1-4 unit Primary (FICO 500-579) | 90% |

### Rate/Term Refinance
| Property | LTV |
|---|---|
| Primary | 97.75% |

### Cash-Out Refinance
| Property | LTV |
|---|---|
| 1-4 unit Primary (owner-occupied ≥ 12 months) | 80% |

### Streamline Refinance
- No appraisal required
- Net tangible benefit required
- Must be current (0x30x6, 1x30x12)

## Credit (II.A.4.b)

### Minimum FICO
- 580+ for 96.5% LTV
- 500-579 for 90% LTV
- Below 500: ineligible

### Derogatory Credit Waiting Periods
| Event | Waiting Period | Extenuating Circumstances |
|---|---|---|
| Bankruptcy Ch 7 | 2 years | 1 year |
| Bankruptcy Ch 13 | 1 year (with court approval) | 1 year |
| Foreclosure | 3 years | 1 year |
| Short Sale | 3 years | 1 year |
| DIL | 3 years | 1 year |

### Collections/Judgments
- **Medical collections:** Excluded from DTI
- **Non-medical collections ≥ $2,000 cumulative:** Must pay off OR include 5% of balance in DTI
- **Judgments:** Must be paid prior to or at closing
- **Tax liens:** Must be paid OR on payment plan (≥ 3 months documented payments)

## Mortgage Insurance (MIP)

### Upfront MIP (UFMIP)
- **1.75%** of base loan amount (all transactions)
- Can be financed into the loan

### Annual MIP
| LTV | Loan Term | Annual MIP | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| ≤ 90% | ≤ 15 yr | 0.15% | 11 years |
| ≤ 90% | > 15 yr | 0.50% | 11 years |
| > 90% | ≤ 15 yr | 0.40% | Life of loan |
| > 90% - ≤ 95% | > 15 yr | 0.50% | Life of loan |
| > 95% | > 15 yr | 0.55% | Life of loan |

*Base loan amounts > $726,200 have higher MIP rates*

## Property Requirements (II.D)

### Minimum Property Standards
- Safe, sound, secure
- Adequate heating, plumbing, electrical
- No health/safety hazards
- Roof with ≥ 2 years remaining life
- No peeling paint (all ages — lead-based paint rules for pre-1978)
- Adequate access (year-round, all-weather road)

### Appraisal
- Full interior/exterior required (no waivers on FHA)
- Valid for 180 days (120 for new construction)
- FHA case number assignment required
- FHA Amendatory Clause and Real Estate Certification required

### Condo Approval
- Must be on FHA-approved condo list OR
- Single-unit approval (SUA) for units in unapproved projects
  - SUA not available for new construction or conversion < 1 year

## Common FHA Gotchas
- **Identity of Interest (II.A.2.a):** LTV limited to 85% for transactions between parties with relationships (family, business) — exceptions exist for tenant-to-owner, employees
- **Flip rules (II.A.2.b):** If seller owned < 90 days, ineligible. 91-180 days: second appraisal if > 100% price increase
- **Non-occupant co-borrower:** Max 75% LTV unless family member
- **3-4 unit self-sufficiency:** 75% gross rent must cover PITIA
- **Student loans:** If deferred, use 0.5% of outstanding balance as monthly payment (or actual payment if documented)
